How to Play

This is a text adventure with a twist: things will happen as you explore, without your control. To get around, you will need to use your wits and keep your fingers ready as you explore Frontera.

For example, typing

inventory

will display your inventory. You can also attack enemies:

attack goblin with knife

You should experiment with different types of commands to see what works, and what's hilarious!
