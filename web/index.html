How to Play

Dungeon of Sombrero is a text adventure game with a multi-player twist. As you explore, you'll run into other players, collect items from dungeons and fallen monsters, and use your wits to survive in combat. To get around, you'll have to keep your fingers ready.

For example, typing

inventory

will display your inventory. You can also attack enemies:

attack goblin with knife

You should experiment with different types of commands to see what works (and what's hilarious)! If you ever get stuck, either look at the Command Reference or you can just type

help

into your console. Good luck, adventurer!

Commands

Go west, west, w
Any of these will move your character in the specified direction.

Look at, inspect, examine
Find out more about the world around you.

Take, pick up, drop
Places an item in your inventory.

Attack orc, attack player
It's clobberin' time.

Give player item, trade with player
Or maybe you're a more charitable sort of adventurer.

Open inventory, inventory, i
Check out your sweet loot.

Health, check health
Keep up-to-date on your health, you only have 100 pts to spare.

And more...
Play around with different commands, and see what happens!

About Us

We're Alex Reinking, Dani Dickstein, and Nick Hathaway, all Yale Computer Science majors. We made Dungeon of Sombrero during YHack2014, trying to spread the love for text-based adventure games in the tradition of Colossal Cave Adventure, Avalon, and Zork.

Text adventures are pretty much the bomb, and we're just hoping we can convince everyone of their ultimate glory.
